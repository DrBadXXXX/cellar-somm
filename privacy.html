Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cellar Somm ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Cellar Somm application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Wine Collection Data. The App stores your wine inventory, tasting notes, purchase history, and related data. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced via Apple's CloudKit to your other devices. We do not have access to your iCloud-synced data.

Community Tasting Notes. If you choose to share tasting notes with the community, those notes (along with a stable anonymous identifier) are stored in our cloud database so they can be displayed to other users.

AI Feature Data. When you use AI-powered features (such as label scanning or wine chat), images or text you submit are sent to third-party AI service providers (e.g., Google Gemini) for processing. We do not store the content of these requests beyond what is needed to deliver the response.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information unless you contact us directly.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • We do not track your location.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features.
  • To display community tasting notes to other users.
  • To improve wine-matching accuracy through anonymized match logs.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal wine data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud container, protected by Apple's security infrastructure. Community data and match logs are stored in Google Firebase/Firestore with standard security practices. We use HTTPS for all network communication.

5. Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete the App. iCloud data is retained according to your Apple iCloud settings. Community notes can be deleted from within the App at any time. Anonymized match logs are retained indefinitely for quality improvement.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Your Rights

You can delete your local data by removing the App. You can delete community notes from within the App. If you need assistance with data deletion, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cellar-somm.com.
